# B2B Lead Generation Volume vs Personalization (2026)
**Expert:** Alex Berman | **Channel:** Alex Berman
**Source Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ikndrts8NQU

---

## 🎯 Key Highlights
- **Targeting beats brute force:** More sends will not fix weak ICP, list hygiene, or offer-market fit.
- **Prove on a slice:** Pilot on a tight, high-fit cohort (~200); scale only when replies prove the motion works.
- **Infrastructure is downstream:** Domains, inboxes, and warm-up amplify a working motion; they do not substitute for it.
